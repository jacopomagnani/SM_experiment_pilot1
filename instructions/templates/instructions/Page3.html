  • In this experiment, you earn points by pairing with other players.
  • Each player is assigned a value and the points you earn when you form a pair depend both your own value and the value of your partner.
  • You cannot directly choose your partner. However, if you wish, you can try to influence the way in which you are paired to others by submitting a bid, which is subtracted from your final points.
