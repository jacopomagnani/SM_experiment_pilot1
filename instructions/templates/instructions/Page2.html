This experiment will consist of two parts.

At the end of the experiment, you will be paid {{ fee }}, plus earnings based on the points you have earned during the experiment. The computer will calculate your average score in Part 1 and your average score in Part 2 and sum them up. This value will then be converted at an exchange rate of AED{{rate_value}} per point and rounded to the nearest integer. To sum up, your final payment is given by the following formula:

{{fee}} + AED{{rate_value}} × (your average score in Part 1 + your average score in Part 2)


Note: in the unlikely event that the sum of your average scores in Part 1 and Part 2 is negative, you will earn {{fee}}.
